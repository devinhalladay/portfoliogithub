physical alphabets

I plasma cut letters out of scrap metal, made letters out of bubble gum, and laser cut letters out of wood.

I then vectorized them and turned them into usable typefaces

Finally, I coded a website where the user is able to type and see different sized via a slider of the typefaces I created.

The dropdown menus have a preview of the font and a button to take you to the page where you can see more of the font and type with it
